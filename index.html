I'm James Sweetman

I graduated with a PhD in Physical Oceanography from the Australian National University, where I worked in the Climate and Fluid Physics Laboratory on ice-ocean interactions through laboratory experiments.

Academic Work

Internal waves

Investigating the Effect of Internal Waves on Vertical Ice Melting Rates

This experimental project represents the first reported research on explicitly investigating the interaction of high-mode internal wave beams on a vertical slab of ice.

Heaving and orbital currents

The Influence of Ocean Swell and Wind Waves on Iceberg Melting

This experimental project builds on previous work on ice melting from wave erosion and unidirectional forced convection.

Double-diffusive convection

Laboratory experiments of melting ice in warm salt-stratified environments

This experimental project extends on previous work of ice melting in salt-stratified environments by varying ambient temperatures.

Technical skills

Here are some of the experimental techniques, methodologies, and scientific skills I've picked up.

Experimental Skills

Quantitative Synthetic Schlieren

Implemented Wildeman's Fourier method to visualise internal waves, meltwater plumes, and other fluid dynamics phenomena.

Optical flow

Utilised optical flow techniques on shadowgraphs and thermographs to analyse fluid motion.

Dye visualisation

Utilised dye visualisation techniques to study mixing processes and visualise fluid dynamics.

Shadowgraphy

Utilised shadowgraphy techniques to visualise ice interfaces, meltwater plumes, and study flow structures.

Light attenuation

Utilised dye and the Beer-Lambert law as a proxy for salt concentration.





Methodologies

Wave generation and control

Developed systems for generating and controlling wave generators in experimental setups with SmartMotors (vertical agitators, horizontal pulleys).

Double-bucket method

Proficient in creating stratified environments through a variety of double-bucket methods (boat floaters, base injection, preheated).

Conductivity and temperature measurements

Managed measurements from a traversable conductivity and temperature probe to determine water column properties.

Stereoscopic imaging

Employed stereoscopic imaging to gain better coverage of full fluid dynamics inside a tank without parallax.





Scientific Skills

Multi-dimensional analysis

Performed multi-dimensional analysis and dimensional reduction on experimental data, including time-series and spectral analysis.

Quality control and assurance

Developed and implemented quality control measures to ensure the accuracy and reliability of experimental data.

Metadata management

Managed metadata for various experimental datasets, ensuring proper documentation and accessibility.

Parallel processing and GPU acceleration

Integrated parallel processing techniques and GPU acceleration within Matlab on local machines to optimize computational workflows and improve performance.
